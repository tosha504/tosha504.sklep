Environmental friendliness

The material does not contain harmful chemical components that could evaporate when the coating is heated. Therefore, it is absolutely safe.

Static neutrality

Unlike many types of artificial materials, wood does not accumulate a static charge and, accordingly, does not contribute to the accumulation of dust on the floor surface.

Thermal insulation

Natural wood has negligible thermal conductivity, therefore, in the cold season it significantly reduces heat loss in the room.

Aesthetics

The expressive texture of the coating allows it to fit seamlessly into almost any interior styles.
